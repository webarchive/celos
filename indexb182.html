Priya Rose joined CELOS in 2002. At present she is in ninth semester of Integrated MSc course in Photonics.

She did her sixth semester project on "Single Beam Mirage Effect".

She has been a member of organising committee for international and national conferences like PHOTONICS 2004 and PLASMA 2005.
