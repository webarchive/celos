Annual Photonics Workshop is organised every year on 27-28 February. Every year these workshops offer insight into some realm of Photonics. The main target group for these workshops are the young researchers and teachers planning to enter these novel and fascinating fields of research.  The focal theme for APW 2005 was Recent trends in light matter interactions and APW 2006 was Ultrafast Optics.
