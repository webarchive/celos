The Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences is on the Trikkakara campus of the Cochin University, which is about 15 kms from the heart of the Cochin city. As one of the largest and most popular cities in Southern India, Cochin is well served by a good transport system. These pages aim to highlight how easy it is to get to Cochin, and inform you about some of the things you can expect to see when you are there. Also you get enough information to get around the campus.
