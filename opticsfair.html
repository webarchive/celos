Home » Events » OPTICS FAIR

 Optics fair is an annual educational outreach program organised jointly by the ISP-CELOS-SPIE and ISP-OSA Student Chapter.The two day event is aimed at providing the students an opportunity to observe and understand experiments that have lead to the formulation of theories in the field of optics.The fair has been structured to address the requirements of students of all age group.There are sections for each category of students : Kids Section (Class III - VII), High School (Class VIII-X), Higher Secondary Section (Class XI-XII).In addition there is a General Section meant for students of all age group.Every year hundreds of school students from in and around Cochin become a part of the event.
