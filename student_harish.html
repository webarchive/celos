Harish Natrajan joined CELOS in 2002. At present he is in ninth semester of Integrated MSc course in Photonics.

He is a native of Cochin itself. His interest areas are Non Linear Optics, Photonic Materials and Quantum Optics. He wants to establish a research career in Photonics.

In sixth semester he undertook a project titled "Crystal Growth and Study of Electro-Optic Effect in Potassium Dihydrogen Phosphate Crystals" for which he won the ISP-SPIE project presentation award.

He is an active participant in various activities of the department as well as ISP-SPIE student chapter. He was in the organizing committee of PHOTONICS 2004, PLASMA 2005 and Annual Photonics Workshop 2004, 2005 and 2006.
