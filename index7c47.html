The cinema is not an art which films life, the cinema is something between art and life.
               -Jean-Luc Godard


When the feeling triggered by cinema lasts beyond momentarily experience, then it is touching your life. Photonium is the fraternity of those young minds, who cherish and celebrate this life touching spirit of cinema.

Photonium was started in late 2005 by a group of seven students (Adil Masood, Sibilathullah AM,  Praveen CA, Anish Krishnan MA, Sreeparvathi Warrier, Gopika Ramanandan and Saurabh Raj ) of CELOS to provide a platform in the campus where the true patrons of this art can explore as well as manifest the enigma of cinema. The aim of Photonium is to promote and popularize the cinema at its best inside the campus. To achieve this goal, Photonium is involved in two kind of activities:

 • To encourage viewing and appreciation of critical cinema, Photonium organizes screening of films related to different aspects of life at regular basis for free. The club also encourages discussions and exchange of views related to the cinema among its members and viewers.

 • To encourage the creativity and innovation t owards the art of cinema, Photonium is involved in campus film production where it produces films and documentaries by harnessing the talent and skills of students only. Photonium has already produced two documentaries in the very short span of about six months.

Einstein's Universe

“Things should be made as simple as possible, but not any simpler”   – Albert Einstein

Simple is beautiful. And no one can frame this essence in any better words than the person who conceived the most beautiful theory of nature. History witnesses, but only rarely, the life of such epoch making individuals.
Realization of a documentary on this millennium man was not just about presenting the facts, but it was about conveying the essence of the spirit immortalized by Albert Einstein.
The spirit of originality.
The spirit of existence
The spirit of persistence
The spirit of simplicity
‘Einstein’s Universe’ is an attempt by those who endured this spirit while indulging in ‘Einstein’s Universe’, to tribute and celebrate the spirit of ‘Einstein’.

Its the very first production of Photonium. In fact, it was during the realization of Einstein's Universe that the idea of evolving an organized platform in the form of Photonium cropped up. 'Einstein's Universe' is unique in many sense as it was made using only archive videos, clips , images, animations and sound tracks. Still it succeeded in conveying its true message in the simplest form. Since then, it has been screened successfully at many academic institutes like IIT Delhi, IIT Madras, VIT Vellore etc as well as in many film festivals. Recently there was a special screening of Einstein's Universe alongwith a presentation on its making at SPIE Annual Meeting at San Diego, USA during 13–17 August, 2006.


A Sentient Sequel

This is the story of a premier Science and Technology University of India which ,while striving to live up to its expectations of the society, has made quantum leaps in its academic pursuits and has spread its wings far and wide to encompass new and emerging frontiers across the academic horizon. It was the second venture of Photonium. After the success of 'Einstein's Universe', Photonium was entrusted to produce a short documentary on Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT). Produced within a short notice, Photonium succeeded in performing all the technical works of shooting, editing, dubbing, voice & music mixing etc within a week.

The documentary was inaugurated by the Hon. Education Minister of Kerala, Mr. E.T. Mohammed Basheer, at International Educational Meet, Kochi, in February '06 and since then, it has been accepted as the official documentary of CUSAT.


Project Picasso
