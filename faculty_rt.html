 Dr. Reethamma Thomas recieved PhD from Cochin University of Science and Technolgy in Spectroscopy.

At present she is a contract reader at the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences. Her areas of interest are Laser Induced Fluorescence and Applied Optics.
