Welcome to the Library of Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences. CELOS maintains a  combined library with ISP where a good collection of monographs and reference materials on photonics and related fields is availiable.

Location: The library is located in ISP building.

Opening Hours: 9.00 AM - 4 PM on university working days; closed on Sundays.

Membership: Membership of the library is open to all registered students, researchers and staff of CELOS. Application for the membership from others may be considered by the librarian. For details please contact the librarian.

CELOS Members: CELOS members are also allowed to use the large collection of books and references in International School of Photonics Library and the CUSAT Central Library. Members must register separately in each library before use. Please contact the librarians for details.

Online journal Subscription: CELOS members has access to lot of online journals through the INFLIBNET.
