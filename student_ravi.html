Ravi is a IXth semester student of 5 Year Integrated M.Sc course in Photonics at Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences (CELOS). He joined CELOS in the 5th batch of the Integrated M.Sc program in July-2006 and is expected to complete the course by April- 2011.  He has been involved in the research activities going on in the department and has also carried out some project works in other institutions.  He was awarded with the ‘Summer Research Fellowship’ by ‘Indian Academy of Sciences’ in the year 2010 to do his project at ‘Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore’ during May-July 2010.

Following are the papers in National / International conferences on his credit:

  • Chemical Route For The Synthesis of Gold Nanoparticles Embedded in PVA Polymer Matrix And Its Nonlinear Optical Characterization;  Ravi Kumar, Giridhar Mishra, Krishna Murari, Misha Hari, Gaurav Sharma, Mathew S, R R Yadav, P Radhakrishnan, V P N Nampoori and Santhi Ani Joseph; “National Laser Symposium-09”, Bhabha Atomic research Centre (BARC), Mumbai, Jan-2010

  • Fabrication And Characterisation of a Hollow Polymer Optical Fibre with dye doped cladding; M. Kailasnath, Ravi Kumar, Krishna Murari, VPN.Nampoori, P. Radhakrishnan; International Conference “Photonics 2008”, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi, Dec 2008  

  • Optical Characterization of ZnO Nanoparticles And Nanorods Prepared By Wet Chemical Technique at Low Temperature; Krishna Murari, Ravi Kumar, M Kailasnath, P Radhakrishnan, VPN Nampoori; International conference “SPIE Photonics West 2010”, San Francisco, California, Feb-2010  

  • Light amplification in a dye doped Graded index polymer optical fibre, M. Kailasnath, Krishna Murari, Ravi Kumar, VPN.Nampoori, P. Radhakrishnan; Submitted to the International Conference “Photonics 2010”, IITG, India

 Below is the list of the Projects he has completed:

  • “Silver Nanostrucutres for Surface Enhanced Raman Scattering Application: Synthesis, Charecterization and Stability Studies”: May-July 2010 Under the guidance of Prof. S. Sampath, IPC Department, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore

  • Preparation of Gold nanoparticles-Liquid suspension and Determination of thermal properties”: May-June 2009 Under the guidance of Prof. R R Yadav, Physics Department (UGC Center of Advanced Studies), University of Allahabad, Allahabad, India

  • “Synthesis and Characterization of ZnO nanoparticles and nanorods” : July- Nov 2008. Under the guidance of Mr. M Kailasnath, International School of Photonics (ISP), CUSAT, Cochin, India

  • “Preparation of Preforms by Teflon technique and drawing of different types of doped and undoped optical fibers” :  2007-08 (part time) Under the guidance of Mr. M Kailasnath, International School of Photonics (ISP), CUSAT, Cochin, India      

 He enjoys doing theoretical works as well as experiments. Recently, he has given a seminar on ‘Quantum Theory and Consciousness’ which was highly appreciated.
