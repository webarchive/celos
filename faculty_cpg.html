Dr. C. P. Girijavallabhan has been the Director of the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences (CELOS) for the term 2003-2008 and also the Emeritus Professor at International School of Photonics, CUSAT. He retired in 2008 from the post of the Director.

He received PhD degrees in 1971 from Kerala University and did postdoctoral research at Southampton University in the United Kingdom.

He was the Dean of Faculty of Technology at Cochin University of Science And Technology during 2003-2007.

He was also a visiting professor at Franhaufer Institute, Freiberg, Germany. He is the founder director of International School of Photonics. His research interests include lasers, laser matter interactions, optoelectronics, molecular physics and solid state physics.

He is the member of the governing council of the Nuclear Science Centre (NSC), India, President of Photonics Society of India and a fellow of the Optical Society of India. He was the Vice President of the Indian Laser Association.
