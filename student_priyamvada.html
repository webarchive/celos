I joined CELOS in 2005. At present I am in my tenth semester of the 5 Year Integrated MSc degree course in Photonics and would be completing my course in May 2010. Currently, I am doing my Master thesis project in IISC,Bangalore.  My work is investigating whether a CD-R sample can be used for Bio sensing and Strain sensing measurements. 

 

My areas of interest are Bio Photonics, Plasmonics, Photonic Crystals and Non Linear Optics. I have done a mini project titled  Photoelasticity for strain measurements during the sixth semester of the course under the guidance of Dr. V.P.N.Nampoori, ISP, CUSAT. I have done another project in TIFR, Mumbai in Mass Spectroscopy. After I graduate, I plan to join a reputed institute for doing my doctoral research.  

 

During the course, I have taken seminars on:

Organic LED
Black Body Radiation.
Photodynamic Therapy  
Bio Imaging
Photoelasticity experiments for measuring stress in different objects.
Photonic Crystals.
Fibre Bragg gratings
Laser applications in medicine etc.
I am a student member of the International Society for Optical Engineering (SPIE), the Optical Society of America (OSA) and IEEE.
