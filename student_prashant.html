 I joined CELOS, Cochin University of Science and Technology in the year 2005 to participate in five year integrated programme in photonics offered by the centre. Presently, I am in ninth semester of the M.Sc course. Being born in rural area of Samastipur, I did my primary and secondary education at boarding  school; a foresight from my parents to help me achieve high academic standards towards a rewarding career. I am hard working and very determined. I enjoy working with a team and am able to work unsupervised. I am reliable, I strive to always keep a positive attitude and have the ability to learn quickly. I am adaptable and can work with people from diverse cultures/backgrounds. 

My research interests are centred on optoelectronics, lasers, nano-photonics, and light matter interaction specifically non-linear optics. I have worked for a semester as well as for two summer internships in projects involving nanoparticles synthesis & characterization, holographic imaging and non-volatile memory devices at reputed labs in the country. After I graduate, I plan to attend a graduate school for doctoral research eventually leading to a research career in photonics.

I have volunteered and participated in many national and international conferences and workshops. Also, the opportunity to attend conferences and courses allowed me with the chance to network with peers. I also take the chance to demonstrate the undergraduate physics at every opportunity, as I enjoy passing on the knowledge and techniques that I have gained to the younger, developing students. I am lively in various activities such as Optics to School, Open House, and various other educational and social outreach programs. I have also volunteered as a science communicator in "science express" which is a state of art science exhibition on train travelling across India.

As if all of that weren’t enough, I play cricket, football, table tennis, badminton etc. When I joined the university, I also joined the university cricket club out of my love for the game and soon became a very keen player for the team. I played for two seasons and have had some glorious wins. I also have the hobby of reading novels, magazines. My leisure pursuit is travelling and I have travelled to quite a no: of places in Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Goa etc.
