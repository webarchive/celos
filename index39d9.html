Club Di Graficia which is better known as Graphics Club in English is the latest club formed by a few CELOS students. This club has been formed as a result of continuous efforts by Anish Krishnan MA. The main objective of this club is to share the knowledge and expertise in the field of Graphic Designing and Web Designing.

In its small span of existence the club has already performed some momentous tasks.

• Designed the ISP - SPIE student chapter website and won the best SPIE student chapter website award for the year 2006.

• Designed the brochure for Cochin University of Science and Technology to be presented at the International Education Meet 2006 at Cochin.

• Designed the ISP - OSA student chapter website.
