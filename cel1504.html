Module I - Matrix formulation of QM: Schrödinger, Heisenberg and interaction Pictures, matrix formulation of linear simple harmonic oscillator, coherent state, number operator, density operator, angular momentum matrices, addition of angular momenta, Clebsch-Gordon coefficients, Pauli’s spin matrices, identical particles

Module II - Time independent perturbation: first order and second order approximations, Stark effect, Zeeman effect, degenerate states, variational method, hydrogen and helium atoms

Module III - Time dependent perturbation: interaction of light with matter, first order approximation, transition probability, dipole approximation, Einstein coefficients, quantization of EMF, spontaneous and stimulated emission,

Module IV - Scattering theory: partial wave analysis, born approximation, scattering by hard sphere and square well, coulomb scattering

Module V - Relativistic QM: Klein Gordon equation, plane wave solutions, Dirac equation, plane wave solutions, positron theory, spin of electrons from Dirac’s theory


References:
1. Quantum Mechanics - LI Schiff
2. Text book of Quantum Mechanics - Thankappan
3. Quantum Mechanics - G Aruldhas (Text)
