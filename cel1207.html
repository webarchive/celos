Module I - Emergence and character of science, the Iron Age, science and industry, development of science in 18th and 19th century

Module II - Science in 20th century, the birth of modern science

Module III - Astronomy in ancient India, Europe, Egypt and other civilizations, some of the astronomical instruments

Module IV - Indian contribution during ancient to medieval period: sulbasutras, decimal system, number representation (various alpha numeric systems), contributions of Aryabhata, Brahmagupta, Varahamihira, Bhaskara
Contribution by Kerala mathematicians during middle age e.g. Madhava, Neelakanda, Jyeshtadeva

Module V - Introducing some of the classic works: Aryabhatiyam, Opticks, Galileo’s Modern Science, Principia of Newton
Topics in Philosophy of science: works of Kanada, Francis Bacon, Thomas Kuhn, Karl Popper


References:
1. Science in History - J D Bernal
2. Science in India (Vol 2) - Indian National Science Academy
3. Golden Age of Indian Mathematics - S Parameswaran
4. Mathematics in Ancient and Medieval India - A G Bag
5. The History of Science and Technology from Ancient Greeks to Scientific Revolution - S Pangenburg, D K Moser
6. Astronomy before the telescope - C Walker
7. Aryabhatiya of Aryabhata - K V Sharma
8. The Sulbasutras - S N Sen and A K Bag
9. History of Technology in India
10. History of Astronomy in India
