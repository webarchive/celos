Dr. Geetha K. is Research Associate at the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences, Cochin University of Science and Technology. She completed her PhD from International School of Photonics, Cochin University of Science and Technolgy in Fibre Optic Sensors and Dye doped Polymer Waveguide Devices in the year 2007. Her areas of interest are fiber optics sensors and amplifiers, waveguide devices, dye doped polymer devices and non linear optics.
