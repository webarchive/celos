Batch - 5

Mobile:  +91-09995238043

Deep Punj is a 7th semester student of 5 Year Integrated M.Sc course in Photonics in Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences (CELOS). He joined CELOS in the 5th batch of the Integrated M.Sc program in July 2006 and is expected to complete the course by 2011.

 His area of interests includes Bio-Photonics, Nano-Photonics,Experimental Condensed Matter and Holography. 

He is a student member of the International Society for Optical Engineering (SPIE), Optical society of America (OSA), Laser & Electro-Optics Society (LEOS).
