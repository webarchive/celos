Sreeraj T P joined CELOS in 2004. At present he is in the eighth semester of the 5 Year Integrated MSc degree course in Photonics and would be completing his course in May 2009.

His areas of interest are Quantum Field Theory, Quantum Optics, Foundations of Quantum mechanics and General Theory of relativity.

He has done theoretical mini project titled " Two photon Interference problem : Quantum Optics" during the sixth semester of the course.

During the course, he has undertaken seminars on:

* Special theory of relativity : Mechanics
* Uncertainty principle and its implications
* Electrodynamics in the light of special relativity
* Moire patterns and surface contouring
* General theory of relativity


He is a student member of the International Society for Optical Engineering (SPIE), since March 2005.
