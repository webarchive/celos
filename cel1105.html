Module I - Probability spaces, conditional probability and independence, random variables and random distributions, marginal and conditional distributions

Module II - Curve fitting and principle of least squares, linear and quadratic curves, simple linear regression and correlation

Module III - Independent random variables, mathematical expectation, mean and variance, binomial, Poisson, and normal distributions, law of large numbers

Module IV - central limit theorem (no proof), sampling distribution and test for mean using t-distribution, χ2 and F distributions
Time series analysis: stationarity and nonstationarity, autocorrelation function

Module V - Testing statistical hypothesis: significance level, Neyman-Pearson theorem (no proof) and some of its simple applications, large sample test, standard error, tests based on t, χ2 and F


References:
1. Probability and Statistics for Engineering and Sciences - J L Devore
2. Probability and Statistics - Schaum Series
3. Fundamentals of Mathematical Statistics - S C Gupta and V K Kapoor
4. Statistical Methods - S P Gupta (Text)
5. Time Series Analysis - G E P Bor, G M Jenkins
