Greshma Narayan did the course in the time period 2002-2007.

She is a native of Cochin itself. Her interest areas are Bio Photonics, Remote Sensing, Optical Sensors, Optoelectronics and Astro Physics. She wants to establish a successful industrial career in Photonics.

She has done a project titled "Photonic Crystals in Nature: Color Production in Peacock Feathers" in the sixth semester of the course.

She has taken seminars on Bio Inspired Materials, Bio Sensors, Optical Nose, Optical Networking, Laser Cooling and Trapping, Polarization Effects in Materials, Sampling Theory, Super Nova, Thin Film Deposition Techniques and Waveform coding Techniques during the course period.

She is a student member of The International Society for Optical Engineering and The Photonics Society of India. She is a regular member for Open House Programs conducted by CELOS for school and college students.

She was a member of organising committee for PHOTONICS 2004 and PLASMA 2005. She also attended the Indo - UK Workshop 2006 on Fiber Optics and its Applications and Annual Photonics Workshop in 2007, 2006, 2005 & 2004 held at ISP, CUSAT, Cochin

She is a regular participant in University Arts and Sports activities.
