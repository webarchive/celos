During the last several years, Photonics has emerged as an area of considerable importance in diverse areas like communication, computing, medicine and entertainment. In view of the growing importance of the subject, International School of Photonics (ISP) and Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronics Sciences (CELOS) of CUSAT in collaboration with City University, London organized an Indo- UK workshop on Fibre Optics and its Applications during August 29-31. The main objective of this Workshop was to give an introduction to various topics in Fibre Optics to motivated students, researchers and teachers who want to enter in this area of Photonics.
