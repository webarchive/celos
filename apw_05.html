The Annual Photonics Workshop 2005 was organized on February 27-28, 2005. The primary objective of APW-2005 was to offer an introduction to some of the areas like Recent Trends in Optics, Optical Communications, Optics of Nanomaterials, Ultrafast Optical Processes, Integrated Optics, to those who are interested in such aspects of light-matter interactions. The main target group was young researchers and teachers planning to enter these novel and fascinating fields of research.
